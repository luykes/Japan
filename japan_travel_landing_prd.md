
# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
## Project: Japan Family Travel Planner Landing Page

### 1. Overview
Build a website landing page that presents a complete Japan travel plan for families. 
The page should showcase an optimized itinerary, cost breakdown, accommodation strategy, 
transport planning, and cost-saving techniques while keeping the trip exciting.

The page will act as both:
- A travel guide
- A planning tool
- A conversion funnel for potential downloadable guides or future travel products

Target audience: Families planning a Japan trip who want to balance cost, experience, and simplicity.

---

# 2. Goals

Primary goals:
- Provide a clear travel plan (Tokyo → Kyoto → Hiroshima → Osaka)
- Show realistic budgeting in AUD
- Provide accommodation strategy
- Avoid tourist traps
- Demonstrate cost-saving techniques
- Inspire excitement about Japan travel

Secondary goals:
- Potential lead magnet for downloadable travel planner
- SEO traffic for Japan travel planning

---

# 3. Target Audience

Primary audience:
- Families (2 adults + kids/teens)
- Budget-conscious travelers
- First-time Japan visitors
- Travelers planning 10–18 day trips

Secondary audience:
- Couples planning Japan travel
- Digital nomads planning extended stays

---

# 4. Core Value Proposition

Key differentiators:

# 10. Cost Saving Techniques

Use airport bus instead of expensive rail transfer.
Stay near major train lines.
Avoid expensive tourist districts.
Use regional rail passes instead of nationwide JR Pass.
Eat local street food and ramen shops.
